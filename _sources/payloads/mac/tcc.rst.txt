Privacy Preferences Policy Control Payload
==========================================

.. figure:: /_static/ProfileManifests/Icons/ManifestsApple/com.apple.TCC.configuration-profile-policy.png
    :align: right
    :figwidth: 200px

The Privacy Preferences payload controls the settings that are displayed in the "Privacy" tab of the "Security & Privacy"
pane in System Preferences.
